In the month of August 2024, more than 50 women workers from Vilacheri, Kalappanpatti, and Kamaatchipuram  of Madurai district, Ondipulinaayakanur of Virudhunagar district and Irulakkudumbanpatti of Dindigul district became members of registered unions by paying Rs 120 as annual subscription. Some of the members also registered themselves with the unorganized workers’ welfare board.

Union officials earlier hand contact meetings with these workers at their villages explaining the nature and function of the unions and the federation. After carefully considering the activities and the purpose of these unions, new members agreed to be members of these unions. 

Public events were held in the villages to show the formal inclusion of the village units into the federation. Officials of the federation and the unions participated in the events explaining publicly the objectives of the federation. In some events there were cultural events to make it a celebration.
